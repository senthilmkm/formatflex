Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. This policy explains how we protect your information.

FormatFlex ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and disclosed by FormatFlex.

1. Information We Collect

Files for Conversion

When you use our service to convert documents, you upload files to our secure servers. These files are processed solely for the purpose of conversion and are legally deleted from our servers shortly after the process is complete. We do not view, share, or store your content permanently.

Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage data to help us improve the app, such as crash reports and performance metrics.

2. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate our app:

  • RevenueCat: Used to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions. They may collect transaction data as per their privacy policy.
  • Expo: Our app is built using Expo, which may collect data to ensure app stability.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your files during transit (encryption) and processing. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access or delete your personal data. Since we do not require user accounts and do not store files permanently, we hold minimal data about you.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
